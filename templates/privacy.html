Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, take exams, contact customer support, or otherwise communicate with us. This information may include: name, email address, profile picture, and any other information you choose to provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. For example, we use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your account;
  • Track your exam scores and progress;
  • Display your profile on the global leaderboard (if applicable);
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this privacy policy or as required by law. We may share aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

5. Your Choices

You may update, correct, or delete information about yourself at any time by logging into your account and modifying your profile or using the "Delete Account" feature. Please note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@medharank.com.
